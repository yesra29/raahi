#  Raahi

**Explore Every City Like a Local**

Raahi is a Flutter application that helps users discover authentic local experiences, famous markets, food spots, hidden gems, and cultural attractions across different cities in India.

The goal of Raahi is to help travelers and locals explore cities beyond the typical tourist attractions and experience places that truly represent the city's culture and identity.

---

##  Features (Planned)

* 🏙️ Discover cities across India
* 🛍️ Explore famous shopping markets and local products
* 🍲 Find popular food spots and hidden gems
* 🗺️ Interactive maps and directions
* ❤️ Save favorite places
* 🧳 Create and manage travel itineraries
* ⭐ Reviews and ratings
* 🔍 Search and filter places
* 📍 Personalized recommendations

## 💡 Inspiration

Raahi was inspired by the idea that every city has its own unique culture, markets, food, and hidden gems that are often difficult for travelers to discover.

The app aims to become a travel companion that helps users experience cities like locals.
